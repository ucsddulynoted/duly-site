AUDITIONS (OPEN)

Passionate to learn and sing?

We are looking for passionate singers and vocal percussionists of any skill level to add their unique voices to our dynamic ensemble. Experience is helpful but not necessary. Whether you have formal training or just sing in the shower, everyone is welcome. Natural talent is valued, so don't hesitate to audition!

Usually held beginning of every Fall Quarter, auditions are open to all UCSD students, so come show us what you've got and be part of a community that values music, creativity, and camaraderie!

Want to Audition? Let's Break it Down:

  1. Book your audition via Calendly: Auditions will last ~15 minutes and slots are available throughout the dates and time periods shown on Calendly. If you cannot make any of the available slots, please contact us through Instagram @ucsddulynoted or email ucsddulynoted@gmail.com and we will try our best to accomodate you.
    *NOTE: Locations will be posted daily on our Instagram.
  2. Choose your song: Pick a song that truly showcases your range, unique sound, and personality!
    *NOTE: Song must NOT be musical theater, opera, or rap.
  3. Show up and sing: Please arrive at least 5 minutes prior to your scheduled time. During your audition, we'll lead you through some scales to warm you up and assess your range. After testing your sight-reading and ear, you'll sing a minute to a minute and a half of your chosen song. *OPTIONAL: You can also do a beatboxing demonstration in which we test your ability to hold a steady beat as well as have you do a 1 minute freestyle!
  4. Expect a Callback: We'll announce callbacks on the final audition day and email more information to those selected. You'll join a mock rehearsal to meet and expereicne singing with our group, allowing us to see how you perform musically! *NOTE: You will be expected to follow show music, but don't forget to have fun!
