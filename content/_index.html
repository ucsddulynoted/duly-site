HI THERE, WE'RE Duly Noted!

Founded in Fall 2012, at University of California, San Diego, we are the seventh of nine a cappella groups on campus. Together, we sing and perform, united by a love of music.

AUDITIONS (CLOSED UNTIL FALL 2025)

Passionate to learn and sing?

We are looking for passionate singers and vocal percussionists of any skill level to add their unique voices to our dynamic ensemble. Experience is helpful but not necessary. Whether you have formal training or just sing in the shower, everyone is welcome. Natural talent is valued, so don't hesitate to audition! Usually held beginning of every Fall Quarter, auditions are open to all UCSD students, so come show us what you've got and be part of a community that values music, creativity, and camaraderie!

Interested in booking Duly Noted for your event?

Duly Noted delivers exceptional performances tailored to any event, and is available to both public and private arrangements. For specific inquiries, please email ucsddulynoted@gmail.com including relevant information in your message.
